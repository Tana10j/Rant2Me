What is Rant2Me?
Rant2Me is an anonymous platform where you can express your feelings, vent frustrations, or talk to supportive listeners without judgment.
Is the service free?
Yes, basic features are free. Premium services like professional listeners requires a plan.
Is my identity protected?
Absolutely. Your anonymity is our priority, you have the right to sign in with whichever ID you want, and our systems only recognize you by your email and user ID which gurantees anonymity.
Do I need to create an account?
Yes, you do need an account to access the website and its features, but we do not require your personal ID.
What are the various plans?
The plans gives you access to the premium experiences of having a one-on-one chat with our trained listeners or therapists. You can still make use of the anonymous chat setup, we can still see your messages there, but we unfortunately cannot respond. It is meant for users to vent and not feel scared because the chat deletes from our system after 5 minutes.
How do I purchase a plan?
You can purchase a plan (either minutes, hour or weekly access) via our secure payment portal or earn them through community participation(Coming soon).
Can I delete my chat history?
You don't need to. We never store your chats. Once the session ends, everything is deleted automatically.
Can I choose who I chat with?
Yes, you can choose categories of listeners (peer, professional, therapist) and even switch during a session.
What if I want to report someone?
You can report a listener or user if their behavior is inappropriate. Our team takes all reports seriously.
Can I use Rant2Me on mobile?
Yes! Our platform is mobile-friendly and works seamlessly on all devices.
