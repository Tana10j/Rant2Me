What is Rant2Me?
Rant2Me is an anonymous platform where you can express your feelings, vent frustrations, or talk to supportive listeners.
Is the service free?
Yes, basic features are free. Premium services like professional listeners require tokens.
Is my identity protected?
Absolutely. We never store chats or personal information. Your anonymity is our priority.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is needed to start chatting anonymously. However, signing up gives you access to more features and tracking tokens.
What are tokens used for?
Tokens unlock premium experiences like one-on-one chats with trained listeners or therapists.
How do I get tokens?
You can purchase tokens via our secure payment portal or earn them through community participation.
Can I delete my chat history?
You don't need to. We never store your chats. Once the session ends, everything is deleted automatically.
Can I choose who I chat with?
Yes, you can choose categories of listeners (peer, professional, therapist) and even switch during a session.
What if I want to report someone?
You can report a listener or user if their behavior is inappropriate. Our team takes all reports seriously.
Can I use Rant2Me on mobile?
Yes! Our platform is mobile-friendly and works seamlessly on all devices.
