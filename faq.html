Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about how Rant2Me works, payments, and your privacy.

What is Rant2Me?

Rant2Me is an anonymous platform where you can express feelings, vent frustrations, or talk to supportive listeners without judgment.

Is the service free?

Yes, basic features are free. Premium services like professional listeners require a paid plan.

Is my identity protected?

Absolutely. We prioritize anonymity. You may sign in with any ID you prefer, and our systems only recognize your email and unique user ID.

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, an account is required to access features, but no personal ID is needed.

What are the various plans?

Plans give you premium one-on-one chat access with trained listeners. The anonymous chat remains free but is for venting only; messages auto-delete after 5 minutes.

How do I purchase a plan?

You can purchase a plan (minutes, hourly, or weekly access) securely via our payment portal. Community rewards are coming soon.

Can I delete my chat history?

You don’t need to, we never store chats. Once a session ends, messages are deleted automatically.

Can I choose who I chat with?

Yes, you can select from categories of listeners (peer, professional, therapist) and switch mid-session if needed.

What if I want to report someone?

You can report any inappropriate behavior. Our team reviews all reports seriously.

Can I use Rant2Me on mobile?

Yes, the platform is fully mobile-friendly and works on all devices.

Still have questions?

Contact our support team and we’ll respond within 24 hours.
